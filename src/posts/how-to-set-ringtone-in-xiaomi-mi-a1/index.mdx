<p>
  Xiaomi Mi A1 is one of the best budget Android smartphone of the year 2017.
  The main attraction of Mi A1 is the stock experience and great camera packed
  in premium body. But the downside of this is lack of software customization
  which is available on other smartphones which comes with MIUI. Phones with
  MIUI has ability to customize everything according user's needs but Xiaomi Mi
  A1's Android One doesn't allow it. Setting a custom Ringtone is a part of
  customizing your smartphone. Today we are going to share with you step by step
  guide on How to set Ringtone in Xiaomi Mi A1.
</p>
<h2>How to set ringtone in Xiaomi Mi A1 without any third party apps</h2>
<p>
  If you want to set custom Ringtone in Xiaomi Mi A1 without any third party
  apps, Then here is a simple method to set Ringtone in Xiaomi Mi A1. It's most
  easy method to set ringtone in 
  <a href="http://amzn.to/2ItKNCO" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">
    Xiaomi Mi A1
  </a>.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>
    1. First, download or save the Ringtone you want to set to your Mi A1.
  </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>2. Now open the pre-installed file manager.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>3. Find the folder where the ringtone is saved.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>
    4. Copy the file by long pressing the file and then tap on copy
    button(overlapping square box button left to three vertical dots.) which is
    at the top. 
  </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>
    5. Now go to the folder Local&gt;Internal storage&gt;Ringtones.
  </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>6. Paste your Ringtone here by taping on paste icon.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>7. Now go to Settings&gt;Sound&gt;Phone Ringtone.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>
    8. Choose your Ringtone from the list which you want it as default phone
    Ringtone.
  </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>9. Click on OK and you're done! The Ringtone will be set!</strong>
</p>
