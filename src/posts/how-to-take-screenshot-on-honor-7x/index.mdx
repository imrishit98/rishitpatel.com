<p>
  Taking a screenshot on Honor 7x is very easy. There are multiple methods to
  take the screenshots on Honor 7x. In this post, I will cover 2 different
  methods of how to take screenshot on Honor 7x.
</p>
<h2>
  <strong>How to take screenshot on Honor 7x using screenshot button</strong>
</h2>
<p>
  Taking the screenshot on Honor 7x using screenshot button is the simplest way
  to take screenshots on Honor 7x. Here is the step by step guide on how to take
  screenshot on Honor 7x using screenshot button.
</p>
<ol>
  <li>Go to the screen which you want to take a screenshot.</li>
  <li>Access quick setting by swiping down from the top of the screen.</li>
  <li>Tap on screenshot button.</li>
  <li>The screenshot will be captured.</li>
  <li>To view the screenshot, go to the Gallery -&gt; Screenshots.</li>
</ol>

<h2>
  <strong>How to take screenshot on Honor 7x using keys</strong>
</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Go to the screen which you want to take a screenshot.</li>
  <li>
    Press and hold both the Power button and the volume down button
    simultaneously for three seconds, or until you hear the camera shutter
    sound.
  </li>
  <li>The screenshot will be captured.</li>
  <li>To view the screenshot, go to the Gallery -&gt; Screenshots.</li>
</ol>
<h2>
  <strong>How to take long screenshot on Honor 7x</strong>
</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Go to the screen which you want to take a screenshot.</li>
  <li>Access quick setting. Tap on the screenshot.</li>
  <li>You will see scroll screenshot button on the preview screen.</li>
  <li>Tap on it. It will start taking scroll screenshot or long screenshot.</li>
  <li>Tapping on the screen will stop taking the screenshot.</li>
</ol>
