<h2>How to take screenshot on Xiaomi Mi A1 using the hardware keys</h2>
<p>
  Taking a screenshot using hardware keys is the most common way of capturing
  screenshot on any Android smartphone which works on Mi A1 well. Here is the
  step by step guide on how to take screenshot on Xiaomi Mi A1 using the
  hardware keys.
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Go to the screen on which you want to take the screenshot.</li>
  <li>
    Press and hold volume down and power button simultaneously for couple of
    seconds.
  </li>
  <li>
    <img
      src="How-to-take-screenshot-in-Xiaomi-Mi-A1.png"
      alt="How to take screenshot on Xiaomi Mi A1"
      width="500"
      height="500"
    />
  </li>
  <li>The screenshot will be captured.</li>
  <li>
    The screenshot will be automatically saved
    in&nbsp;Photos-&gt;Album-&gt;screenshot.
  </li>
</ul>

<h2>How to take screenshot on Xiaomi Mi A1 using menu option</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Go to the screen on which you want to take the screenshot.</li>
  <li>Now drag notification bar from top to bottom and find Toggles.</li>
  <li>Next locate the screenshot icon.</li>
  <li>Simply tap on the icon to perform screenshot.</li>
  <li>The screenshot will be captured.</li>
  <li>
    The screenshot will be automatically saved
    in&nbsp;Photos-&gt;Album-&gt;screenshot.
  </li>
</ul>
<h2>How to take screenshot on Xiaomi Mi A1 using the Google Assistant</h2>
<p>
  Xiaomi Mi A1 is a Android One device so it comes with Google Assistant. You
  can take screenshot using the Google Assistant by following steps given below.
</p>
<ul>
  <li>
    Go to the screen on which you want to take the screenshot and&nbsp;open
    Google Assistant.
  </li>
  <li>
    You can do this by saying “OK Google” or by long press on home button.
  </li>
  <li>Now either you can say “Take screenshot” or type.</li>
  <li>Screenshot will be taken.</li>
  <li>
    Do note that, if you are taking screenshot using this method, it will not be
    saved automatically.
  </li>
  <li>You need to either share or upload it to the Google photos.</li>
</ul>
