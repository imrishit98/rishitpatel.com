<h2>
  How to take a screenshot in 
  <span class="black">Moto E4 Plus using hardware keys</span>
</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Go to the screen on which you want to take the screenshot.</li>
  <li>
    To the right side of your phone , Press and hold the volume down button and
    power button at the same time for couple of seconds.

  <li>The screenshot will be captured.</li>
  <li>

    in File Manager) automatically.
